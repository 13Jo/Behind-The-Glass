What We're About

A gallery first and a comic book store second, we celebrate all aspects of nerd culture. We strive to provide a supportive and inclusive atmosphere, and welcome any and all into our little corner of the world.

Our aim is to show and sell comic art and illustration pieces, and to expose our community to the work of both well-known and emerging artists and illustrators from all over the country.

"It first started as a dream, a goal, an ambition. Now, it has grown into a community which we want to expand and continue to support."

The Store

We offer hundreds of back issues of rare, interesting and off-the-wall titles, and provide reasonable and fair grading and pricing. We might also be interested in buying your comics and toys. Don’t be afraid…just ask.

The Museum

Check out our small-toy and comic art museum in the back of the store. Years of collecting and connecting has given us the ability to display some of our favorite toys and artwork. There are stories behind almost every piece.
